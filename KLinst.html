This lookup table is being built out of several sources: Klallam Word Of The Day - WOTD, Klallam Dictionary - KD, literature such as Lambert's 'The 7 Brothers of the House of Ste-Tee-Thlum', Tim Montler's http://klallam.montler.net, and other sources as I find them.  As of 7/24/18 there are about 3500 entries.

1. Clicking in the text box above makes an on-screen keyboard appear.  The little box beside it lets you choose between English and Klallam.

2. To choose Klallam you would choose the pulldown item 'clm - KlallamU', or to enter in English (default), choose English.

3. Enter the word you wish to search for.  It can be either in English (such as 'salmon'), or Klallam (such as 'kʷítšən'), or a category (such as 'Fish').

4. Click on the 'Submit' button.  If your word was found, a listing will appear in this window.  If not, the whole 3500+ words will appear!
